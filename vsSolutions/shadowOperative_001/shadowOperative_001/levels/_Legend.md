##	LEGEND
---
###	0-9
- 0 = Empty/Void/Null/Air
- 1 = Floor
- 2 = Wall_Left
- 4 = Wall_Right
- 5 = Corner_TopLeft
- 6 = Corner_TopRight
- 7 = Corner_BottomRight
- 8 = Corner_BottomLeft
- 9 = Filler

###	A-Z
- D = Door (Closed)
- E = Enemy Spawn
- P = Player Spawn
- T = Enemy Path
- B = Battery

###	a-z
- d = Door (Open)
- s = Switch/Button
- w = Window/Glass
